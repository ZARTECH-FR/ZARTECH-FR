### 💡 Core Services & Expertise

We provide high-level technical leadership and end-to-end architecture services:

* 📐 **Technical Scoping & System Design:** Conducting feasibility studies, technical framing ("Cadrage"), and designing scalable, future-proof architectures.
* 🏗️ **Azure Cloud Architecture:** Designing secure, highly available, and cost-effective cloud-native infrastructures exclusively on the Microsoft Azure ecosystem.
* 📝 **Architecture Documentation:** Producing comprehensive technical documentation, including High-Level/Low-Level Design (HLD/LLD), Architecture Decision Records (ADRs), and detailed DATs (Dossiers d'Architecture Technique).
* 🤝 **Consulting & Tech Mentorship:** Accompanying and guiding technical teams, auditing existing systems, and bridging the gap between business and IT.
* 💻 **Web & Mobile Platforms:** Overseeing the development of high-performance custom applications (iOS, Android, Web) tailored to your architecture.
